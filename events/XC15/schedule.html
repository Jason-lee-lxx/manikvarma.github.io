The workshop venue is Room 511f on level 5 of the NIPS convention centre. Participants at this year's workshop will have an option of staying back during the lunch break for John Langford's Vowpal Wabbit 8.1 tutorial from 13:15 - 14:15. This is an additional feature not directly related to the workshop, though John might touch upon extreme multi-class algorithms, and participants are free to choose between lunch and the tutorial (fast eaters can choose both!)
